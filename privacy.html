Privacy Policy for DueVault

Effective date: June 6, 2025

DueVault stores your data (customers, products, transactions, and shop settings) locally on your device using SQLite. In addition, your data is automatically backed up to Google Firebase Firestore when you log in, add or modify data, and periodically in the background. This ensures you can restore your data on any device by signing in with the same Google account.

Data we store in the cloud: Customer names, mobile numbers, unique IDs, balances, credit limits, transaction history, product lists, and app settings (shop name, phone, language, currency).

Authentication: We use Google Sign-In to identify you. We only receive your email address and basic profile information; we never store your password.

No access to your Google Drive: DueVault does not read or write to your Google Drive. All cloud data is stored exclusively in Firebase Firestore, Google's secure cloud database.

Data retention & deletion: Your cloud data remains associated with your Google account. If you wish to delete all your data, contact us. Uninstalling the app does not automatically delete cloud data.

By using DueVault, you agree to Google's Privacy Policy and Firebase Privacy Policy.
